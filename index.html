Hey, I'm Qaisar.

I am a PhD Researcher in Computer Science at the University of Turin, Italy. My work focuses on the intersection of Deep Learning and Hardware Acceleration, specifically targeting energy-efficient deployment of Neural Networks on FPGAs for embedded computer vision.

More about me View Publications

About Me

Currently, I am pursuing my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Idilio Drago. My research addresses the trade-offs between performance and resource utilization for real-time AI applications, exploring hardware-software co-design, quantization, and reconfigurable logic.
